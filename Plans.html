Plans and Progress

Our plan for this project will be illustrated below:

  • The mobile app will serve as the interface for the users
  • Users can interact with the app, customise settings based on their needs.
  • User selects a time for the alarm to go off, similar to traditional smartphone alarms. But the innovation of this project is the users can adjust the robot’s speed, the volume when the alarm goes off, and the users cannot turn off the alarm by using the smartphone but only by catching and turning it off directly with the robot.
  • Users can decide how the robot will operate when the alarm goes off with the second feature.

Through each step in the development of the project, our members encountered several problems and needed to make decisions for strategy. One possible difficulty was the robot’s movement to make sure it works normally without hitting obstacles and suddenly appearing confusing for users. So that we need to continuously improve their sensors and algorithms. Another significant challenge could be choosing appropriate modules for our project. For example, the communication between the app and the robot using Bluetooth or Wi-Fi were still considered, but after finding and discussing their topic, we decided to choose Bluetooth because of the compatibility and dependability of both Android and IOS smartphones that could not be affected by other Wi-Fi connections. Therefore, we need more feedback when going into practice for the development of the robot through each version.
