Communications

For the group communication process, we have connected other teams’ members through social media by:

  • Facebook Messenger where we will share information about the project, for example suddenly coming up with an interesting idea or having our own problems on the project and arrange any available meeting schedule, so we are accessible to answer any questions immediately.
  • Group meetings on Teams can be prepared where we contribute in-depth feedback together on the project ideas and work more efficiently and faster in two sessions per week regardless of geographical distance between us.
  • Use Google Doc and GitHub to upload member’s reports and code for checking information and duration on commit branches which can easily be fixed.

Each team member is asked to contribute their ideas proactively with other mutual respect. In case some members do not respond to communication, we will remind them continuously in every forum and if not submitted by the allotted time, so they get low marks for contribution because the group's rules were previously approved by all members. Fortunately, with the high collaboration and productivity between us, we have successfully finished the project according to the common goal.
