1 Project manager You have responsibility for synthesizing and observing the overall project process by dividing tasks based on each member’s strengths, seeking investors, managing the budgets and resources. Also, you will work together with each member to understand their strengths and weaknesses and guarantee the project stays on schedule. + Degree in project management, or related field
+ Prior experience in real project roles
+ Strong communication and teamwork skills and better decision making, conflict-solving
+ Effectiveness in managing resources and finances…
2 Robotics Engineer In our project, you need to build up and normalize the alarm clock robot. It is compulsory to work with software and mechanical teams to implement your robotics technology with robot’s functionality. Your responsibilities will be selecting and carrying out different types of modules based on our budgets and resources, developing the algorithms to manipulate the robot's movement. + Degree in robotics engineering, or related field
+ Experience in robotics fields with knowing module concepts and algorithms for designing robot’s structure
+ Good at programming languages
+ Excellent communication and teamwork abilities….
3 Software Developer Your obligation will establish a mobile application on smartphone which can connect to the alarm clock robot. You need to work closely with other teams to integrate the function of the robot that can connect between the app and the robot. You will design the user interfaces with basic functionality, create the customization options as well as integrate connections for the management. + Degree in software engineering, or related field
+ Experience in mobile application development for designing user interfaces and interactions and managing data storage
+ Good at programming languagea
+ Excellent communication and teamwork abilities….
4 Mechanical Engineer Your role needs to create the physical structure of the alarm clock robot. It is necessary to cooperate with other teams to ensure stable operation on every usability, dependability and manufacturability of the robot . You will create 3D designs, manage and test the prototype, and do evaluations. + Degree in mechanical engineering, or related field
+ Experience in mechanical development software (ex. AutoCAD, 3D printing)
+ Familiarity with mechanical analysis and material choices
+ Excellent communication and teamwork abilities….
