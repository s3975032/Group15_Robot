Aims

Our goal is to design and build a robot that compels the user to be more active upon waking up. The robot would wander about to escape the user turning it off until the user caught it, at which point it would switch off. Our concept is to develop and produce a robot that compels the user to be more active upon waking up.

Not only can the user get some early morning exercise with our concept of an intelligent alarm clock robot that they have to chase around in order to turn off, but the action of chasing along with the loud sound from the robot can also awaken the user's senses and collect oneself from the fatigue of waking up in the morning.

  • Waking consumers up in a hurry way by going beyond the usual beeping sound of alarm clocks and using innovative and interesting new ways to wake them up.
  • Creating an application for a smart reminder clock that enables users to schedule reminders and alarms for themselves.
  • Being a better alternative than phones for waking up and going to sleep in this day and age, when living in the 21st century requires utilising technology all the time.
  • Providing customers with a comprehensive set of tools for enhancing the quality of their sleep, such as a programmable alarm clock with a variety of functions.
